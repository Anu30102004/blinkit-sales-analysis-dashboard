## 📌 Project Overview
 
This project is an interactive sales analysis dashboard built in **Power BI** for **Blinkit** — India's leading quick-commerce grocery delivery platform. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, outlet analysis, and product insights to support data-driven business decisions.
 
---
 
## 🎯 Business Problem
 
Blinkit operates across multiple outlet types, sizes, and locations. The business needs to understand:
 
- Which outlets and item categories generate the most revenue?
- How does fat content preference affect sales?
- What is the relationship between outlet size and total sales?
- How have sales trended over the years since outlet establishment?
---
 
## 📊 KPIs Tracked
 
| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Total Sales** | Overall revenue generated across all outlets |
| **Average Sales** | Average revenue per transaction |
| **Number of Items** | Total count of different items sold |
| **Average Rating** | Customer satisfaction score across outlets |
 
---
 
## 🔍 Dashboard Features
 
- **Fat Content Analysis** — Comparison of Low Fat vs Regular product sales
- **Item Type Breakdown** — Sales distribution across product categories
- **Outlet Size Analysis** — Performance by Small, Medium, and Large outlets
- **Outlet Location Tier** — Sales by Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities
- **Outlet Type Comparison** — Supermarket vs Grocery store performance
- **Yearly Trend** — Sales growth since outlet establishment year
- **Interactive Slicers** — Filter by outlet size, location, and item type
- **Field Parameter Toggle** — Switch between KPIs dynamically

## 📁 Dataset Details
 
- **Source:** BlinkIT Grocery Sales Data
- **Format:** Excel (.xlsx)
- **Key Columns:**
  - Item Fat Content, Item Type, Item Visibility
  - Outlet Size, Outlet Location Type, Outlet Type
  - Outlet Establishment Year
  - Sales, Rating
---
 
## 💡 Key Insights
 
- **Tier 3 cities** contribute significantly to total sales despite lower visibility
- **Regular fat content** products outsell Low Fat products across most categories
- **Medium-sized outlets** generate the highest overall revenue
- **Supermarkets Type 1** lead in total sales among all outlet types
- Customer ratings remain consistently high (~3.9 avg) across all outlet types
